## Dashboard Views

### 1. Executive Summary View
High-level overview for decision-makers. Includes:
- KPI cards (Total Revenue Proxy, Avg Discount %, Avg Rating, Total Products)
- Category treemap by revenue proxy
- Discount vs Rating scatter plot
- Top 10 products by purchased_last_month

### 2. Operational Drill-Down View
Detailed exploration for category managers. Includes:
- Interactive filters: product_category, price_band, discount_tier, is_best_seller
- Product-level detail table with sorting and search
- Price band distribution bar chart
- Discount tier performance comparison
